# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Credit compliance officers, model risk managers, and legal counsel checking machine learning models against CFPB/OCC regulations under strict regulatory oversight.

## Product Purpose
AVARENT Meridian serves as a high-fidelity compliance platform. It provides automated monitoring of credit decisioning systems, audits algorithmic drift (AIR and SPD), quarantines correlated alternative data features, debiases training sets via generative models, and seals critical decision evidence in an immutable, cryptographically signed ledger.

## Brand Personality
Professional, authoritative, resilient, and objective. Built as a command-center interface to convey high precision, absolute compliance readiness, and trust.

## Anti-references
AI slop templates (generic blue-to-purple gradients, card lists with colorful side-stripe accents, redundant/monotonous layouts, decorative glassmorphism, flat scales, or empty state illustrations).

## Design Principles
* **Regulatory Precision**: Strictly display direct, granular decimal values (Adverse Impact Ratio and Statistical Parity Difference) with exact thresholds instead of simplified percentages.
* **Ledger Continuity**: Emphasize immutable, chronological audit trails with cryptographically sealed tags ("Audit Sealed") to show complete historical reliability.
* **Contextual Transparency**: Make deep feature relationships and metrics collapsible or accessible on demand (such as the Feature Dependency Graph) to allow deep inspection without interface clutter.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Adheres to strict WCAG 2.1 AA contrast standards, keyboard-accessible quick navigation, responsive vertical stack for density scaling, and minimized motion settings support.
